Date

  • No pipes: {{today}}
  • Formatted using pipe: {{today | date}}
  • {{today | date:'fullTime'}}
  • {{today | date:'full'}}
  • {{today | date:'w'}}

Case

  • {{name | uppercase}}
  • {{name | lowercase}}
  • {{name | titlecase}}

Currency

  • {{amount| currency}}
  • {{amount| currency:'EUR'}}
  • {{amount| currency:'GYD'}}
  • {{amount| currency:'RUB'}}

Decimal

  • {{amount | number:'1.2-6'}}
  • {{1.0 | number:'1.2-6'}}

Percent

  • {{percentage | percent:'.2-2'}}

Custom Negate Pipe

  • {{amount | negate }}

Custom String Pipe

  • {{name | stringManip }}
  • {{name | stringManip:'reverse' }}
  • {{name | stringManip:'encrypt' }}
